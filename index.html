About Me.

Full Stack Web Developer

Greetings! I'm Daniel Tisue a Software Engineer with a keen interest in art, design, and photography. Originally, a chemistry major, I ventured into programming to craft my own photography portfolio website, sparking a fulfilling career.

In 2018, I channeled my diverse interests into dynamic projects as a Freelance Full Stack Web Developer. My commitment to robust design and prioritizing user oriented features created seamless interfaces. I also cultivated strong client relationships, consistently surpassing expectations and deadlines.

In 2019, I was hired through Aquent recruiting agency to work for Texas Instruments. During my tenure as a Web Developer/Email Production Manager, my role extended beyond development as I led impactful IT solutions for backend processes, transforming workflows and establishing consistent SLAs. This comprehensive approach not only met but exceeded expectations, earning recognition for elevating project quality.

In 2022, at CoreLogic, I played a pivotal role in ensuring the reliability of existing projects. Collaborating seamlessly with cross-functional teams, I contributed to innovative modernization efforts through refactoring, integrating new technologies (including cutting-edge AI solutions), and comprehensive testing. The projects not only enhanced performance and maintainability but also proved valuable to the sales strategy at CoreLogic. My knack for providing innovative solutions not only improved efficiency but also contributed to forward-looking strategies, driving impactful outcomes.

Beyond web development, I enjoy movies, skateboarding, and fooling myself into long stretches of attempted bodybuilding. I am also a devoted husband and father.
