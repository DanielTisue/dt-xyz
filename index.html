About Me.

Full Stack Web Developer

I'm Daniel Tisue and I have spent the last 3.5yrs dedicated to perfecting the craft of building complex modern websites. I enjoy learning and solving complex problems.

Originally, my path led me into the field of chemistry. Shortly after, I pursued sales. I used the knowledge aquired from both professions to get a taste of wordpress web development in 2015 when I decided to start my own e-cig company.

In 2018, I was interested in starting my own business again and decided to learn how to build my website from scratch. I landed my first client at the end of 2018. I was hired in 2019 as a contract developer to Texas Instruments where I still work today. While at Texas Instruments, I was able to shape processes within in our team to improve efficiency. TI and other contract work has given me the opportunity to build a wide variety of websites using tech stacks such as html, css, javascript, express, mongodb, ejs, and react.

Oustide of web devlopement, I enjoy movies, skateboarding, and fooling myself into long stretches of attempted bodybuilding.
